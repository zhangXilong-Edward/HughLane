Artists' Takeover: Renata Pękowska

Renata Pękowska is a visual artist and researcher based in Dublin. Her background includes architectural studies, craft design and contemporary art theory.

Artists' Takeover: John Byrne

John Byrne has a background in performative work including The Border Interpretative Centre 2000 (recently hosted as part of Worlds Without End: Stories Around Borders at the Hugh Lane Gallery)

Artists' Takeover: Helena Gouveia Monteiro

Helena Gouveia Monteiro is a visual artist and experimental filmmaker from Portugal. She received her MFA from the Villa Arson Art School in Nice in 2015 and lives and works between Dublin and Marseilles.

Artists' Takeover: Dor Guez

Dor Guez will be in conversation with Sara Reisman, This conversation will explore themes arising from Dor Guez’s work currently on view in Worlds Without End: Worlds Without End

Artists' Takeover: Elaine Byrne

Elaine Byrne is an artist whose practice and enquiries have centred on topography and the marks that delineate identities and legacies, using photography, video and sculpture.

Artists' Takeover: Ashleigh Downey

Ashleigh Downey is an environmental artist living in Ireland. Formally trained as a textile designer, from Central St. Martins, London (1995-98).

Artists' Takeover: Michelle Hall

Michelle Hall is a member of the Hugh Lane Artists, Guides and Lecturers panel. She is a visual artist working across various disciplines including moving image, photography, text and performance.

Artists' Takeover: Niamh McCann

McCann’s work is a considered, individual voice in contemporary Irish art; effortlessly correlating strands of three dimensional work, painting/drawing and installation.
