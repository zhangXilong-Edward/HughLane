Support

Hugh Lane fuels imaginations all over the world. With help from generous friends like you, we are able to share our collection and programs with millions of visitors from around the world each year, helping the widest possible audience understand and enjoy modern and contemporary art. From award-winning online and on-site educational offerings, to innovative exhibitions, to free programs that enable a child’s first experience with modern art, your support makes a difference every day.

There are many ways to give to Hugh Lane Gallery you are an individual, a family foundation, a corporation, or somewhere in between—with many custom benefits and privileges available in recognition of your generosity. We thank our members and donors for their loyalty and enthusiasm, and look forward to working with new friends as we build for a future of even more extraordinary art and experiences.
