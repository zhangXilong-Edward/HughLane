Access and Facilities





Information Desk located on the right on entrance to the Gallery.

Toilets
Accessible and standard toilets are located on the ground floor entrance hallway and on basement level.
Baby changing facilities are available on both toilets.

Seating
Folding seats are available, just ask a member of staff or at the Information Desks.

Lifts
A large lift beside the reception area, which provides access to all floors
A small lift in the new wing corridor that provides access from the ground floor to the first floor exhibition galleries

Cloakroom located in the basement that is accessible via steps or the lift.

Family Tours





Why not bring your kids to our kids club!

The collection has been explored by children aged three to adults in their eighties during successful and popular programmes. This new programmed, multi-purpose space allows for additional experimentation.

See lots of our lovely art idea films on the Gallery's video presentation.

For details of upcoming art workshops for children, the Gallery's Education Programme pdf is available here.

Where advanced booking is required or where there is a workshop fee, places are booked through eventbrite.ie.

Guided Tours




Guided Tour
Guided tours can be booked by prior appointment with the Gallery’s Education Department, at €30 per group up to a max of 30 per group.

For further information, please click here.

Self-guided Tour
Self-led group visits to the Hugh Lane are free.

However groups must remain under the supervision of their group leaders and follow all regulations of the gallery and directions of gallery attendants.

Please contact the Education Department in advance of visiting the gallery on a self guided tour to ensure that the visit does not clash with other pre-arranged events stating in your email that you are intending to visit on a self guided visit.

Group Visits
Due to reduced capacity during refurbishment, all tours or large groups should be booked in advance (even when a guide is not required).
